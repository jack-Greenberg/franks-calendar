How to Publish Events

You can publish an event to the Frank’s Calendar platform in one of two ways:

  1. Filling out this form to manually enter event information
  2. Sending an ical (.ics file) to frankscalendar.olin@gmail.com

How to send an ical from your personal calendar:

  1. Open the event into full editing mode in your personal calendar. Focus on the panel labeled “Guests” in Google Calendar or “Invite attendees” in Outlook Calendar. If you can’t edit the correct fields, go to “Sharing on behalf of someone else”
  2. In the text box, type frankscalendar.olin@gmail.com and press enter.
  3. Save the changes.
  4. Check your email. You should shortly receive an email with the subject “Please select a category for the event you have submitted.” Use the magic link inside to go to the event editing portal.
  5. Select the most appropriate category and make sure that all of the fields are filled. See the Filter Guide to help you determine which category is best.
  6. Make sure to click the “Save changes” button!
  7. Keep an eye on your inbox. You should get a confirmation email when your event is approved and made visible on the Frank’s Calendar site. If there is a problem with your submission, a moderator should email you about it within 24 hours.

Publishing on behalf of someone else with Google Calendar:

  1. Click the event to open the pop-up. Click the three dots in the upper right hand corner.
  2. Select the “Copy to” option and a calendar of your choice.
  3. This should open an event editing screen. Now return to the original steps.

Publishing on behalf of someone else with Outlook Calendar:

  1. Click the event to open the pop-up. Click the expand option in the upper right hand corner.
  2. Click the 3 horizontal dots on the navigation bar. Select the “Copy to” option and a calendar of your choice.
  3. This should open an event editing screen. Now return to the original steps.
