What belongs on Frank's Calendar?

Frank’s Calendar puts campus events in one place, and should be used to share events with the Olin community that are also being shared via other channels (carpe emails, flyers around campus, etc).

Since Olin is a busy place and it would be impossible to capture everything happening on campus, Frank’s Calendar only advertises community-wide events.

These belong

  • Events public to the Olin community (current students, faculty and staff)
  • Events hosted by a member of the Olin community

These don't

  • Events only open to a smaller group of people (e.g. club meetings only for club members, parties with your friends)
  • Events hosted by non-Oliners

Moderator System

Calendar moderators maintain the events on the calendar to guarantee that Frank’s Calendar is populated with accurate, up to date information about what is happening on campus. They also monitor events to make sure they meet the requirements of what is okay to share.

Their job and admin privileges include:

  • Reviewing event submissions
  • Approving, requesting changes, or cancelling an event submission
  • Sharing the link to edit your event with you if you lost it
